The Vocal Ranger

This tool helps find the vocal range of a guitarist.

First, start in the original tuning of the song you wish to play.

Try to sing along with the track.

If it seems like you are having trouble hitting the high notes, bring the tuning down half a step and try again until you feel comfortable singing the song.

Have fun and rock on!
